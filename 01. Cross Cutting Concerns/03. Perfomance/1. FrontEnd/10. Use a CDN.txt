

10. Use a CDN

•	Serve static assets (JS, CSS, images) from a CDN for faster delivery.
---
11. Prefetch and Preload Resources
•	Use <link rel="preload"> and <link rel="prefetch"> for critical resources.
---
12. Avoid Blocking the Main Thread
•	Offload heavy computations to Web Workers if needed.
•	Avoid large synchronous operations in the main thread.
---
13. Enable HTTP/2 or HTTP/3
•	Ensure your server supports HTTP/2 or HTTP/3 for multiplexed, faster resource loading.


14. Monitor and Analyze Performance
•	Use Lighthouse, Web Vitals, and React Profiler to measure and track performance.
---
15. Progressive Web App (PWA)
•	Consider making your React app a PWA for offline support and faster repeat visits.
---
